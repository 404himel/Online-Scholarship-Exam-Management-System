Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to apply for the scholarship exam?
Students from the following five schools are eligible to apply: School 1, School 2, School 3, School 4, and School 5.
How can I apply for the scholarship exam?
You can apply online through our official website by filling out the application form and submitting the necessary documents.
What subjects are included in the exam?
The exam includes subjects like Mathematics, Science, English, and General Knowledge.
How will I receive my exam results?
You can check your exam results on our website by entering your registration number.
Is there any exam fee?
No, the scholarship exam is completely free for eligible students.
What are the benefits of the scholarship?
Selected students will receive financial support for their education, along with mentorship opportunities.
